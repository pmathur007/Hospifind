Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, many hospitals have exceeded their treatment capacities and suffer from a lack of resources. Hospifind helps you choose the best hospital in the area that will be able to treat you based on a variety of factors. Hospifind works not only to help "flatten the curve" and spread out coronavirus infections over time, but also to "raise the line" that symbolizes healthcare system capacity. Fill out the form below to get started. #raisetheline
